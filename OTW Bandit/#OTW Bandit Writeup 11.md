#OTW Bandit Writeup: 11

Objective: Get a flag that is rotated 13 positions in the alphabet

This type of encryption is called ROT13, to solve it I use [cyberchef](https://gchq.github.io/CyberChef). You could also use tr to undo the rotation but it is a lot more time consuming.
